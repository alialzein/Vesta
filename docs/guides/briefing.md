# Briefing — your daily intelligence, picked for you

The **Briefing** page gives you a short, personalized daily briefing from the
real world: the topics, clients, competitors, and regulations *you* chose to
follow. It's not generic news — every item answers three questions: **what
happened, why it matters to you, and what to do about it.**

One rule never changes: **work comes first.** The briefing lives on its own
page and never appears above Today's Radar.

## Where to find it

Left sidebar → **Intelligence → Briefing**.

## First time: tell Vesta what to watch

The first visit asks what you care about (this takes a minute):

1. **Topics** — tap the suggestions (AI and technology, Cybersecurity,
   Regulations…) or add your own, as broad or precise as you like:
   - `UAE data privacy law`
   - `Microsoft 365 Copilot updates`
   - `Construction industry procurement`
2. **Companies to track** — your company, key clients, competitors, vendors.
3. **Region focus** (Worldwide, UAE, Lebanon…), **items per day** (5–15), and
   **style** (short executive summary or more detail).
4. **News engine** — where the news comes from:
   - **Google News** (default): free topic feeds, fast and reliable.
   - **AI web search**: the AI searches the web itself — costs a little more
     per day, and quietly falls back to Google News if unavailable.
5. Hit **Save & build my briefing** — your first briefing appears in seconds.

## Reading the briefing

- Built **once per day** automatically (first visit of the day) and cached —
  it refreshes after your midnight (Settings → Timezone). **Refresh** rebuilds
  it on demand.
- The **top story** appears as a large featured card; the rest follow in a
  grid. Cards show the **article's own image** when the publisher provides
  one; otherwise a colored category panel stands in (some sites block
  previews — that's them, not a bug).
- Each card shows: a category chip (**Must know**, Regulation & risk,
  Clients & competitors…), the de-clickbaited headline, a short factual
  summary, **Why it matters** written for *your* role and interests, a
  **suggested action** when there is one, and the **source link** — always,
  with the publisher's logo next to the source name.
- **The "% match" number** is Vesta's relevance score: how closely the story
  matches *you* — your role, topics, and tracked companies. 100 means
  "you'd regret missing this". Hover it for the explanation in-app.
- **Save** keeps an item on the **"Saved for later" shelf at the bottom of
  this same page** (it survives across days — read it whenever you have
  time). **Dismiss** removes it. Stories you've already been shown won't
  repeat on later days.

## What it costs and what it shares

- One small AI call per day to rank and personalize (visible in the admin AI
  page under **brief**); AI web-search mode adds one more.
- **Privacy:** only your chosen topic keywords are used to search news —
  **nothing from your mailbox is ever sent to news services.** Every item
  shows its source; Vesta never presents unverified news as certain.

## Quick start

1. Open **Briefing** → add 3–5 topics + your top clients/competitors → Save.
2. Tomorrow morning: read the briefing after clearing your radar.
3. Save anything worth acting on; dismiss the rest — the picks get sharper as
   your topics get sharper (edit them anytime via **Preferences**).
