# Vesta on your phone — install it like an app

Vesta works as a real phone app: install it on your home screen, navigate with
a bottom tab bar, scan a dense app-style list, and act on any item from a
slide-up sheet. No app store needed — it installs straight from your browser.

## Install it on your home screen

**iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open your Vesta address in Safari and sign in.
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square with the arrow).
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen** → **Add**.

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open Vesta in Chrome.
2. Tap the **⋮** menu → **Add to Home screen** (or tap the "Install app"
   banner if Chrome offers one).

**What you get:** a Vesta icon (the blue flame) on your home screen that opens
**full-screen** — no browser address bar, no tabs. It's always live: it shows
the same real data as your laptop, never a stale copy.

## The bottom tab bar

On the phone, navigation lives at the bottom, under your thumb:

- **Today** — the dashboard with the brief and Today's Radar.
- **Inbox** — your synced conversations.
- **Vesta** (the raised blue bubble in the middle) — the full Ask Vesta chat.
- **Briefing** — your daily news briefing.
- **Menu** — everything else (Waiting on you, Draft Replies, Hidden,
  Memory & Rules, Weekly Review, Settings).

## What's different on the phone

- **The radar is a dense list** — each item is one compact row (score, sender,
  title, due), like a mail app, so five or six items fit one screen. **Tap a
  row** and an action sheet slides up with the full summary, *Why this
  matters*, and all the actions: Mark done, Dismiss, Snooze, Draft reply,
  Memory, Activity.
- **The Morning Brief starts compact** — headline, the live numbers, and the
  "Start here" pick. Tap **"Full brief & actions"** to read the whole AI brief
  and reach Clear My Day / Draft Replies.
- Everything else works exactly like the desktop: nothing sends without your
  approval, and anything you do on the phone shows up on your laptop instantly
  (it's the same live Vesta).

## Quick start

1. Install Vesta on your home screen (steps above).
2. Open it in the morning → read the headline → tap **Start here**.
3. Tap any radar row → act from the sheet (Done / Snooze / Draft reply).
4. Need Vesta's opinion? Tap the blue **Vesta** bubble and ask.
