# Weekly Review — your week at a glance

Weekly Review shows you what actually moved in the last 7 days: what you finished,
how many replies you sent, how much noise you dismissed, and which people took the
most of your attention. It's the proof that the system is working — and a quick way
to spot who keeps filling your plate.

## Where to find it

Left sidebar → **Intelligence → Weekly Review**.

## What you'll see

- **Four numbers at the top:**
  - **Completed** — items you marked done this week.
  - **Replies sent** — emails you approved and sent through Vesta.
  - **Dismissed** — items that turned out not to need you (cleared as noise).
  - **Inbound emails** — everything that hit your mailbox this week, for scale.
- **Your week, day by day** — a small bar for each of the last 7 days showing how
  many items you completed. The line underneath tells you how many items carry
  over into next week.
- **Completed this week** — the list of what you finished, with its category and
  when you closed it.
- **Who took your attention** — the senders behind this week's inbound mail,
  busiest first. If one name keeps topping this list, that's a conversation (or a
  delegation rule) waiting to happen.

## How to make the numbers move

The review reads from the same actions you already do on the dashboard:

1. **Mark items done** on Today's Radar (the ✓ on a card, or "Mark done" in the
   right rail) — each one lands in **Completed** and in that day's bar.
2. **Approve & send replies** from the draft composer — each send counts under
   **Replies sent**.
3. **Dismiss** what doesn't need you — counted separately, so "busy work cleared"
   never inflates "work finished".

If the page says **"Your first review is brewing"**, nothing has synced or been
resolved in the last 7 days yet — connect your mailbox (Settings) and clear a few
items, then come back.

## Good to know

- All numbers come only from **your own data** — your items, your drafts, your
  mailbox. Nothing is estimated or made up; an empty week shows as empty.
- The window is **rolling 7 days**, so checking on Friday vs Monday compares the
  same length of week.
