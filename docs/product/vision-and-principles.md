# Product Vision and Principles

## Product name

Working name: **Vesta**

Product category: **AI Chief of Staff / Manager Command Center**

## Vision

Vesta helps managers stop scanning inboxes and start making decisions.

It turns Outlook emails, Teams messages later, reminders, manual tasks, draft replies, and AI-detected commitments into one prioritized command center.

The dashboard should answer:

```txt
What must I handle now?
Who is waiting for me?
What decision is blocking others?
What can I delegate?
What can the AI draft or prepare for me?
What did I promise and forget?
```

## Product principles

1. **Decision-first, not inbox-first**

   - The app should show decisions, blockers, and next actions, not raw message volume.

2. **Every priority needs a reason**

   - A manager must understand why an item is urgent.

3. **Human approval for risky actions**

   - AI drafts and recommends. The manager approves before sending or changing important rules.

4. **Memory must be visible and editable**

   - The manager can teach the assistant, review memories, and delete them.

5. **Reduce time, not add another system**

   - Every screen should help clear work faster.

6. **Portability from day one**
   - The project must not depend on one email address. It should be possible to reconnect or transfer to another mailbox/account later.

## MVP success sentence

The MVP succeeds when the manager says:

> I no longer need to scan my inbox to know what matters today.
